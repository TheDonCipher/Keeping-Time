# THE SOUNDSCAPE SOURCEBOOK: TECHNICAL OUTLINE
## From Documentation to Product

The goal is to package the `REPOSITORY` files into a "Player's Handbook" style guide for writers, roleplayers, and lore-hounds.

---

### 1. The Physics Engine (The Mechanics)
- **Source**: `01_POWER_SYSTEM.md`, `the_score_mechanics.md`
- **Output**: Formalized rules for "Signal Combat."
- **Key Feature**: "System Modes" (Safe Mode, BIOS, Dissonance) as difficulty levels or environmental hazards.

### 2. The Atlas & Faction Guide
- **Source**: `02_FACTIONS_AND_POLITICS.md`, `04_LOCATIONS_ATLAS.md`
- **Output**: Detailed maps and social hierarchies of Ouroboros.
- **Key Feature**: "The Circle of Fifths" as a character progression/alignment system.

### 3. The Gear Catalog (The Armory)
- **Source**: `07_TECHNOLOGY_AND_GEAR.txt`
- **Output**: Detailed stat blocks for Decks, Jacks, and illegal Black Market tech (Echo Jars, Phase Gloves).
- **Key Feature**: "Heat Damping" as a resource management mechanic (replaces MP/Mana).

### 4. Character Registry (The NPC Gallery)
- **Source**: `03_CHARACTERS_REGISTRY.md`, `VOICE_GUIDE.md`
- **Output**: Deep-dives into Taro, Voss, Hana, and the Faction Leaders.

---

### COMMERCIALIZATION PATH:
- **Phase 1**: Digital PDF release on **Itch.io**.
- **Phase 2**: "System Reference Document" (SRD) for other creators to build within the Ouroboros world.
- **Phase 3**: Physical Hardcover "World Bible" via Kickstarter.
