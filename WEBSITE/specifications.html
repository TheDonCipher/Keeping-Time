CORE_SPECIFICATIONS

DESIGN_PHILOSOPHY

Unison was conceived as the ultimate expression of societal Harmony. The design objective was the elimination of social friction—crime, dissent, and unproductive individualism—through passive resonant reinforcement. The entire city is a single, massive musical instrument.

The central spire acts as a primary tuning fork, broadcasting a constant, low-amplitude Harmonic chord. Every building, street lamp, and public transport system is designed to vibrate at a sympathetic frequency, amplifying and propagating the chord throughout the city.

BEHAVIORAL_IMPACT

The ambient Harmony frequency subtly influences the Bio-Plasma fields of all inhabitants. It does not control thought, but rather encourages emotional and psychological states conducive to cooperation and placidness.

The frequency promotes the release of oxytocin-analogous neurochemicals, reduces aggression, and discourages strong, non-conformist expressions of will by making them feel 'dissonant' or 'unpleasant'. The result is a population that is remarkably peaceful, productive, and content.

EFFECTIVENESS_ANALYSIS

From a technical standpoint, Unison is a resounding success. It achieves its design goals with chilling efficiency. The structure perfectly maintains its intended frequency, and the behavioral modifications are stable and predictable.

Note: Critics argue that Unison does not create harmony, but merely enforces conformity. By smoothing the 'sharp edges' of individuality, it may also be stifling creativity and the potential for growth that arises from conflict.
