THE UNFINISHED SCORE

A History of Battles That Never Ended

Authored by the Archivists of the F-Custodians, Cycle 7.4

History is not merely a record of victories and defeats. It is a complex tapestry woven from intent, action, and consequence. Within the Resonance Matrix, where consciousness itself is the medium of conflict, the consequences of battle can linger indefinitely. This text serves as an academic inquiry into the most notable of these phenomena: the "Unfinished Scores." These are not mere battlefields, but locations where the clash of Bio-Plasma fields was so intense, so fundamentally opposed, that the conflict never resolved. Instead, it became a standing wave of dissonant reality, a scar on the soundscape that continues to resonate centuries later. We study these failures not to assign blame, but to understand the profound cost of irresolution and the terrifying power of a note held too long.

Case Study 1: The Glass Duet of Kael and Serah

Location: The Shattered Plaza, formerly the central nexus of Old Aethelburg.
Date of Incident: Cycle 3.12, Day 15.
Primary Combatants: Kael Unison (The Unison Court), Serah Fifthstep (The Vanguard Spiral).
Resulting Anomaly: A Resonant Stalemate; localized temporal and physical crystallization.

Abstract

The confrontation between Kael Unison and Serah Fifthstep is perhaps the most tragic and well-documented Unfinished Score. It represents a perfect ideological and resonant clash: the absolute stasis of Harmony pushed to its limit against the unending forward momentum of Rhythm. The result was not victory for either, but the creation of a permanent, unstable paradox that has rendered the heart of a once-great city uninhabitable.

Resonant Analysis

Kael Unison, embodying the core tenet of his faction, sought to impose a perfect, singular Harmony upon the city's chaotic frequencies. His Bio-Plasma field exhibited unprecedented Coherence, a flawless, unwavering note intended to subsume all others into a state of placid order. His technique was a masterful expression of Control, shaping the very soundscape into a rigid, crystalline lattice.

Conversely, Serah Fifthstep, a prodigy of the Vanguard Spiral, championed the principle of perpetual change. Her power was rooted in Rhythm, a relentless, adaptive pulse that sought to break any pattern and drive evolution. She utilized a high-Amplitude Push, a percussive force designed to shatter Kael's static constructs. Her frequency range was immense, constantly shifting to find a weakness in Kael's monolithic defense.

"Order without change is a tomb. Change without purpose is a storm. I offered a living dance; he demanded a flawless statue. The world was not strong enough for both." - Attributed to Serah Fifthstep, from a fragmented data-ghost.

The Unfinished Score

As their powers met, they created a feedback loop of immense energy. Kael's Harmony would attempt to "freeze" Serah's Rhythm, and her Rhythm would "shatter" his Harmony. This occurred on a micro-second scale, billions of times. Instead of one overcoming the other, the energies became entangled. The result is a zone where time and space are "stuttering." Buildings appear to shatter and reform in a slow, silent rhythm. Figures of combatants, caught in the initial clash, are visible as crystalline statues that seem to shift and change their pose over hours or days. The air itself hums with a deeply unsettling, unresolved chord that can induce severe Desync in any Consciousness Engineer who enters. The "Glass Duet" is a battle that is still being fought, every moment of every day, by the resonant ghosts of its participants.

Lingering Dangers

The Shattered Plaza is a Level-5 Exclusion Zone. The constant, unresolved frequency clash creates "Resonant Shrapnel"—bursts of pure, chaotic energy that can cause immediate Bio-Plasma Burnout. The temporal instability can trap individuals in localized loops. Furthermore, the area acts as a beacon for dissonant entities drawn to the chaos. No attempt to "silence" the zone has been successful; doing so would be akin to trying to silence a star by shouting at it. It stands as a permanent monument to the danger of absolute conviction.

Case Study 2: The Choir of Madness

Location: The Sunken Cathedral of the Choral States.
Date of Incident: Circa Cycle 2.8.
Primary Combatants: The Choral States Collective vs. an unknown Noise entity/ideology.
Resulting Anomaly: A Self-Sustaining Psychic Maelstrom.

Abstract

The fall of the Choral States' capital serves as a grim warning about the vulnerabilities of collective consciousness. By linking thousands of minds into a single, harmonious whole, they created a resonant entity of immense power. However, this unity also created a single point of failure. When a powerful Noise frequency was introduced, it did not just disrupt their Harmony; it inverted it, turning their song of unity into a feedback loop of psychic screaming that continues to this day.

Resonant Analysis

The Choral States operated on a principle of massed Harmony. Thousands of citizens, all trained in basic frequency control, would lend their Bio-Plasma fields to a central "Conductor" caste. This created a field of unimaginable scale and stability, capable of terraforming landscapes and enforcing a placid existence. Their weakness was a lack of adaptability and a suppression of individuality, which they saw as dissonance.

The aggressor is not fully known, but evidence points to a practitioner of pure Noise—not as chaos, but as anti-information. This "Static" frequency was designed to find the resonant weak points in a complex system and amplify them. It did not seek to overpower the Choir, but to introduce a paradox it could not resolve. It was a virus of sound.

The Unfinished Score

The Static frequency was introduced into the collective. Instead of being smoothed over by the overwhelming Harmony, it was amplified. Each of the thousands of minds in the Choir tried to "correct" the dissonance, but in doing so, they focused on it, giving it more energy. The central Conductors lost control, and the collective consciousness turned inward on itself. The song of unity became a cacophony of every suppressed fear, doubt, and selfish impulse from its millions of members, all amplified and broadcast in a continuous loop. The Cathedral, their seat of power, sank into the earth under the sheer psychic pressure.

Lingering Dangers

The Sunken Cathedral is now a source of "psychic plague." The broadcast frequency doesn't cause Burnout, but a far more insidious form of Desync. It slowly erodes an individual's sense of self, replacing their thoughts with the fragmented fears and desires of the long-dead Choir. Explorers report hearing thousands of whispering voices that slowly become their own inner monologue. The area is a testament to the idea that unity without resilience is a fragile shell, and that a single, perfectly placed lie can destroy an empire of truth.

Appendix: Case Study 00 - The Glass Labyrinth of Echoes

Status: Unresolved.
Hazard Level: Omega.

The conflict now known as the Cacophony of the Chimes occurred in the crystalline canyons of the Shardlands. The belligerents were the Chorus of Concord, a Harmony-focused sect believing in the forced unification of all consciousness, and the Shatter-Tribe, a nomadic group who practiced a radical form of Noise as a means of individual liberation. The Chorus sought to impose a single, perfect harmonic onto the region, transforming it into a resonant amplifier for their philosophy. The Shatter-Tribe resisted, not by countering with a coherent frequency, but by introducing chaotic, disruptive Noise patterns designed to break any emergent harmony.

"They sought to make us all sing one note. We chose to scream every note at once, so that none could be heard above the glorious din of freedom." - Last recorded transmission of Grol, Shatter-Tribe chieftain.

The result was a catastrophic failure for both. The Chorus's powerful, structured Harmony wave did not collapse; instead, it fractured. The Shatter-Tribe's Noise did not dissipate; it was captured within these harmonic fragments. The two frequencies became locked in an unstable, repeating loop. The battle never ended; it was frozen. The canyons now form a labyrinth of invisible walls, where moving from one zone to another means crossing a boundary into a violently different resonant state. The air itself seems to chime with dissonant, crystalline tones—the "unfinished score" of their battle.

Resonant Hazard Analysis

The Glass Labyrinth is a textbook example of a perpetual, high-amplitude Desync field. Any Consciousness Engineer entering the area finds their Bio-Plasma field violently pulled in multiple directions. The stable standing wave of their consciousness is battered by the warring frequencies. Prolonged exposure guarantees catastrophic Desynchronization, as the individual's core frequency is literally torn apart. The danger is not from an enemy, but from the environment itself, a battlefield where the weapons are still firing centuries later. The only way to traverse the Labyrinth is to achieve a state of near-perfect internal Coherence, a feat few can manage, making the region effectively impassable and a monument to ideological intransigence.

Chapter 1: Sustainability Violations - Resonant Theft

The core tenet of sanctioned Resonance Matrix manipulation is that the Engineer is a standing wave, a self-contained system. Power is drawn from internal clarity and projected outward. This is a lie of limitation, designed to keep the masses docile.

True power lies in understanding that the Matrix is a shared resource, and any resource can be taken. The following techniques are deemed "unsustainable" because they disrupt the so-called "natural order" and draw power from external sources, often without their consent.

Technique 1.1: Frequency Siphoning

Description: A rudimentary but effective method of draining the Bio-Plasma field of another Engineer. Instead of generating one's own Amplitude, the user attunes their frequency to a target's, creates a resonant bridge, and then deliberately introduces a state of lower energetic potential in their own field, causing the target's energy to flow "downhill."

Execution: Requires a high degree of Coherence to maintain the bridge. The user must match the target's core resonant signature, often by analyzing their breathing and heartbeat. Once locked, the user visualizes an empty space within their own core, a vacuum. The Matrix abhors a vacuum. The target's energy will bleed across the bridge.

Consequences: Causes rapid Burnout in the target. For the user, the influx of "foreign" resonance can pollute their own Bio-Plasma field, leading to long-term Desync and potential personality fragmentation. The stolen energy is never as "clean" as one's own. It's like drinking saltwater to quench thirst.

The "cost" is a lie. The Black Sun is not an end, it's a battery. They just don't want you to have the key. This is the key.

Chapter 2: Forbidden Overdrives - The Black Sun Kiss

Progression through the Sephirotic Gates is meant to be a journey of internal integration and external trial. This is the slow path. The quick path involves rituals that force open the gates, tearing through the fabric of the self to grasp at power.

The most forbidden of these is the "Black Sun Kiss," a ritual that bypasses the higher Sephiroth entirely and draws power directly from the conceptual end-state of the universe—the "Black Sun" where all frequencies are ultimately reallocated.

Ritual Mechanics:

The ritual requires three resonant witnesses whose frequencies are harmonically opposed to the practitioner's. The practitioner must first enter a state of controlled Burnout, pushing their Bio-Plasma field to the absolute brink of collapse. At the precipice, instead of pulling back, they must attune themselves to the concept of absolute zero, of Silence.

This acts as a lure. The practitioner's near-death state creates a resonance with the ultimate entropy of the Black Sun. The witnesses act as anchors, forming a triangular gate that prevents the practitioner's consciousness from being immediately extinguished. For a brief moment, a channel is opened. The practitioner does not gain clarity or insight; they are flooded with raw, unformed potential—the energy of things ending.

Consequences: Catastrophic. The power gained is immense but corrosive. It is the frequency of decay. The user's Bio-Plasma field becomes permanently tainted, slowly eroding their connection to the living Matrix. They no longer "sing their note"; they become an echo of the great Silence. The witnesses are often consumed in the process, their resonance used as fuel to close the gate. Failure results in immediate and total reallocation to the Black Sun, not even leaving a frequency ghost.

This is how you win. Not by shouting louder, but by making it so they can no longer speak. Silence is the final word.

Chapter 3: Weaponized Entropy - The Mechanics of Silence

The ideology of Silence is not merely a philosophy of nihilism; it is a branch of applied resonant science. While natural Silence is the heat death of a frequency, weaponized Silence is an active, predatory force that erases other frequencies.

Device Profile: The Null-Wave Bomb

The Null-Wave Bomb does not explode. It implodes. At its core is a stabilized quantum singularity, resonating in perfect anti-phase with the local universal constant. Upon activation, it does not release energy; it absorbs it. It is a resonance sinkhole.

The device broadcasts a "wave of anti-information." Any Bio-Plasma field it touches is not disrupted or damaged; its core frequency is canceled out. The standing wave that constitutes the individual's consciousness is flattened. Their "note" is erased from the Score.

The effect on victims is absolute. There is no ghosting, no reallocation. From the perspective of the Resonance Matrix, the individual never existed. The area of effect becomes a "Dead Zone," a patch of reality where the Matrix is thin and unresponsive. New frequencies struggle to form there. It is the ultimate act of "sustainability violation"—not just stealing from the system, but deleting a part of it permanently.

Deployment of such a weapon is considered a crime against existence itself. The schematics are believed to be held only by the highest echelons of the Flux Syndicate and a rogue cell within the Artificer Axis.

FIELD NOTES ON RHYTHM

"Everyone's got a plan until they get Pushed in the face. Listen up, rookie. This might keep you from ghosting on your first run." - Old Man Cadence

So, You're a Rhythm Runner?

Good. Melody is for show-offs, Harmony is for statues, and Noise is for psychos. Rhythm is the street. It's the beat of the fight, the pulse of the city. It's about timing, momentum, and hitting hard when it counts. But don't get cocky. Rhythm can run away from you just as fast as it can carry you. Here's the dirt you won't learn at some fancy Vanguard Spiral academy.

The Three-Count of a Fight

Every scrap, from a back-alley brawl to a full-on incursion, has a beat. Sync, Push, Control. That's the mantra. But here's what it really means:

  • SYNC: This isn't about meditation. It's about listening. Feel the rhythm of your opponent. Are they aggressive? A fast, staccato beat. Are they defensive? A slow, heavy four-four time. Syncing is about getting on their wavelength so you can predict their next move. Rookie Mistake: Trying to Sync while you're also trying to Push. You can't listen and shout at the same time. Take a breath, find the beat, then act.
  • PUSH: This is the fun part. The hit. For a Rhythm fighter, a Push is a percussive strike. A shockwave. A sudden, brutal change in the beat. Don't just throw energy. Time it. A Push that lands on the off-beat, right when they're shifting their weight or starting their own attack, is ten times more effective. It's not about raw Amplitude; it's about impact.
  • CONTROL: This is what keeps you from burning out. Control is your stamina bar. For us, it's about maintaining your own rhythm under pressure. When a Harmony-freak tries to lock you in place, you use Control to keep your internal beat steady. When a Noise-fiend throws chaos at you, you use Control to hold onto your simple, powerful two-step. Rookie Mistake: Pushing too much, too fast. You'll drain your Control and be left gasping. A good Rhythm fighter can keep a steady beat for hours. A bad one is a firework: bright, loud, and gone.

Common Ways to End Up as Static

Seen it a hundred times. Don't be the next one.

  • Ignoring Your NAI: That little voice from your Neural Interface isn't just nagging. It's monitoring your Bio-Plasma. When it says "Amplitude nearing red-line," it means it. Listen to it.
  • Feedback Fixation: You land a good Push, they stumble. The feeling is a rush. You want more. So you Push again, harder, faster. You get caught in your own feedback loop, pouring more and more of yourself into it until... pop. You're a ghost. Learn to strike, then reset. Find your rhythm again.
  • The Harmony Trap: A Unison Court enforcer isn't going to trade blows with you. They'll create a field of perfect, boring order. It feels like moving through molasses. The rookie mistake is to fight it, to Push against the whole field. You'll exhaust yourself. The pro move? Don't fight the field, find a new rhythm inside it. Use small, sharp, internal Pushes. Create a counter-rhythm they can't smooth over. Be the drummer in the library.
  • Forgetting to Run: You are not a hero from a ballad. You are a Consciousness Engineer. If you are outmatched, if your Control is failing, if you are Desyncing—get out. A retreat isn't a loss. It's a strategic pause. A rest. You can't win the next fight if you don't survive this one. The best Rhythm runners know exactly when the beat says it's time to go.

Stay sharp. Keep the beat. And don't die.

THE ARCHITECT’S INDEX

A Compendium of Resonant Structures

Foreword: The Resonance Matrix is not a phenomenon confined to living beings. It permeates all matter. Strategic arrangement of mass, material, and geometry can shape the Matrix, creating structures that passively or actively influence the Bio-Plasma fields of their inhabitants. This index profiles significant works of Resonant Architecture, analyzing their design, function, and impact. The morality of such creations is beyond the scope of this technical analysis.

Entry 4.1: Unison Hall (Active Civic Pacification)

Designation: Structure-Class Harmony

Primary Architect: The First Chorus Council
Core Material: Bio-Crystalline Lattice, Tellurium-doped Quartz
Resonant Principle: Forced Harmonic Amplification
Status: Decommissioned (See Historical File: "Choir of Static")

Unison Hall was conceived as the ultimate expression of the Harmony philosophy: a city built as an instrument. Its purpose was to create a passive, pervasive field of emotional and psychological stability, promoting social cohesion and eliminating dissent. The design was based on a series of concentric rings, each tuned to a specific harmonic of a central "Civic Anthem."

Functional Analysis:

The city's central spire, The Conductor, housed a massive, slowly rotating crystalline core. A dedicated choir of Harmony adepts would continuously "sing" the Civic Anthem into this core. The core's geometry and material composition were designed to filter out any dissonant overtones, purifying the Melody before amplifying it and broadcasting it through the very foundations of the city. The streets, buildings, and public spaces were all constructed with specific materials and at specific angles to act as passive repeaters for this signal.

The intended effect was a subtle but constant pressure on the Bio-Plasma fields of the inhabitants. Frequencies associated with anger, rebellion, and radical individualism would encounter constant damping interference, making them difficult to sustain. Conversely, frequencies associated with compliance, contentment, and community were amplified. The architecture did not control thoughts, but it made certain thoughts significantly easier and more pleasant to have than others.

The failure of the project was not in its execution, but in its vulnerability. A system designed for pure Harmony had no defense against pure Noise. The introduction of a chaotic, anti-pattern signal did not just disrupt the Anthem; it used the Hall's own amplification system to create a catastrophic Feedback loop, resulting in the structure's current status as a Resonance Scar.

Entry 4.2: The Luthier's Axis (Failed Global Tuner)

Designation: Structure-Class Megaproject (Failed)

Primary Architect: Master Luthier Corin
Core Material: Phase-Carbon Monofilament, Supercooled Resonant Helium
Resonant Principle: Planetary-Scale Frequency Modulation
Status: Shattered. The "Axis Debris Field" is a navigational hazard.

The Luthier's Axis was arguably the most ambitious architectural project ever conceived. It was not a building or a city, but a single, planet-spanning structure: a space elevator with a resonant tuner at its apex in geosynchronous orbit. The architect, Corin, believed that the planet's own resonant frequency was drifting, causing an increase in conflict and Desync events. His Axis was designed to be a planetary "tuning fork."

Functional Analysis:

The tower itself was a marvel, a cable of woven phase-carbon designed to be perfectly inert. The true mechanism was the orbital station at its peak. This "Tuner" was a massive toroidal chamber filled with supercooled helium. By applying precise electromagnetic fields, the helium could be made to resonate at any desired frequency.

The plan was to have the Tuner broadcast an incredibly subtle, low-amplitude sub-harmonic frequency down the Axis tower. The tower would act as a waveguide, grounding the frequency into the planet's crust. This would, in theory, gently nudge the entire planetary soundscape back into what Corin deemed "perfect pitch." It was environmental engineering on a scale that bordered on reality-editing.

The project failed during its initial calibration test. The complexities of the planetary soundscape had been catastrophically underestimated. When the first test frequency was broadcast, it created unforeseen resonance with several large iron deposits in the planet's mantle. This generated a massive, low-frequency Feedback wave that surged back up the Axis tower. The energy overload shattered the Tuner station and caused the tower itself to whip and then snap. The resulting debris, still charged with chaotic resonant energy, now forms a dangerous and unpredictable belt in low orbit. The failure serves as the ultimate cautionary tale about the hubris of attempting to control a system one does not fully comprehend.

THE CIRCLE AND THE FRACTURE

A Collection of Factional Philosophies

The Perfect Note

By Proctor Valerius of the Unison Court

They call us tyrants. They mistake order for oppression, and peace for stagnation. They are children, screaming for the freedom to play in a collapsing house. They do not understand that a symphony is not composed of a thousand soloists, each playing their own tune. That is not music; it is noise. A symphony is composed of many instruments playing one composition, their individual voices sublimated to create a beauty greater than any one of them could achieve alone.

This is our goal. A society that is a perfect, harmonious chord. We do not seek to erase the individual's note, as our detractors claim. We seek to help them find their proper place in the score. Is the violin oppressed by the fact that it does not play the part of the cello? No. It fulfills its purpose, and in so doing, contributes to a transcendent whole. Through our guidance, through the gentle shaping of our shared soundscape, we eliminate the jarring notes of crime, of poverty, of war. We offer a world without conflict, a society of serene and elegant purpose.

Our critics see a cage; we are building a concert hall. Their blindness is that they cannot imagine a freedom greater than their own ego. They do not see the schism in their own hearts—the desire for peace warring with the desire for selfish distinction. We have resolved this schism. We have chosen peace. It is the only logical choice.

The Ascendant Melody

By First-Chair Elara Jet of the Vanguard Spiral

The Unison Court speaks of a perfect note. There is no such thing. Music is not static; it is movement. It is tension and release, dissonance and resolution. It is a journey. To seek a single, perfect, unchanging chord is to seek the death of music itself. It is to seek Silence.

We of the Vanguard Spiral believe in the forward path. The purpose of consciousness is to grow, to change, to become more complex and more capable. The Resonance Matrix is not a finished composition to be perfectly performed; it is an unfinished score, and it is our right and our duty to write the next verse. Each individual is a soloist, and their life is a Melody. The goal is not to blend in, but to compose a song so powerful and unique that it changes the soundscape for all who follow.

Our weakness, if it can be called that, is our impatience with the past. Tradition is the anchor that holds the ship in the harbor when there is a new world to discover. Our internal struggles are not of philosophy, but of tempo. The followers of Rhythm urge a steady, powerful advance, while the disciples of Melody seek explosive, individual breakthroughs. Both are correct. Both are necessary for the climb. The Court wants to lock the door to the next Sephirotic Gate; we exist to kick it down.

Beyond the Flawed Instrument

By Lead Designer T-9 of the Artificer Axis

Our colleagues in the other factions squabble over the best way to play an instrument that is fundamentally flawed. The "Bio-Plasma field," this wetware of consciousness, is a fragile, inefficient, and unpredictable medium. It is prone to Desync, Feedback, and Burnout. It is limited by emotion and the frailties of the flesh. To base a society on its mastery is to build a house on sand.

The Artificer Axis posits a simple, elegant solution: build a better instrument. Through our Decks, our Jacks, our Neural-Acoustic Interfaces, we are not merely using the Resonance Matrix; we are upgrading it. We are replacing the imprecise, organic standing wave with the clean, logical perfection of the digital signal. We offer an end to the limitations of biology. We offer precision, control, and power beyond the wildest dreams of any "natural" Engineer.

They accuse us of hubris, of abandoning our humanity. This is a failure of imagination. We are not abandoning it; we are transcending it. Our blind spot is a calculated risk: the potential for systemic failure is greater, but the potential for advancement is infinite. Our schism lies between the Integrationists, who see our technology as a bridge to enhance the organic, and the Synthetics, who believe the final goal must be the complete replacement of the "wetware." Both agree on the destination: a future where we are the composers of reality, not just players within it.

The Strength of Many Voices

By Speaker Anaya of the Green-Valley Chorus

The Court seeks one voice. The Spiral seeks one hero. The Axis seeks one machine. All of them are wrong. All of them lead to a single point of failure. Strength is not found in a single, perfect note, but in the rich and complex texture of a thousand voices singing in concert. This is the principle of the Choral States.

We are not a monolith. We are a decentralized network of communities, each with its own song, its own rhythm. We find our Harmony not through enforcement, but through consensus. We listen. We adapt. When a new voice joins, the choir shifts to accommodate it, creating a new, richer chord. Our power comes from our resilience and our adaptability. A single dissonant note can shatter the Court's crystal palace. A single hero's fall can break the Spiral's charge. But to silence the Choral States, you would have to silence every one of us, in every valley and on every street corner.

Our critics call us slow, indecisive. And it is true, consensus takes time. This is our great internal debate: how much dissonance can the choir tolerate before the song is lost? How do we guard against a voice that seeks not to harmonize, but to dominate from within? We have no perfect answer, because to have one would be to abandon our core belief. We choose the difficult, messy, beautiful process of listening over the simple, brutal efficiency of command.

The Beautiful Chaos of Silence

An Anonymous Pamphlet, found pasted to a wall in the Undercity

All systems are lies. The Unison Court's "Harmony" is a cage. The Vanguard's "Progress" is a treadmill for the ambitious. The Artificer's "Logic" is a digital prison. The Choirs' "Consensus" is the tyranny of the majority. They are all just different arrangements of bars, different melodies played to keep you marching in line.

They all fear one thing: the moment the music stops. The moment of pure, unstructured potential. The moment of Noise. We in the Flux Syndicate do not seek to build a better system. We seek to shatter all systems. We are the dissonance in the chord, the static on the broadcast, the silence that proves the song was never necessary in the first place.

Our purpose is to introduce chaos. To break the patterns. To remind the powerful that their control is an illusion. We have no leaders, only cells. We have no grand plan, only targets. Our blind spot? We cannot build. To build is to create a new system, and all systems must be broken. Our schisms are our strength. We are a thousand different factions of one, united only by our desire to see the towers fall and the symphonies end. When the world is quiet, then everyone will finally be free.
