THE_VIRTUOSO
Radical Individualism in the Key of Power
"The world is a stage, and most are merely providing the ambient drone. We are the soloists. We are the ones who define the frequency."

THE_PATH_OF_MELODY

The Virtuoso believes that power is an art form. They reject the collective "mush" of Harmony and the mindless drone of Rhythm. For them, the Melody is everything.

Each Virtuoso is their own empire. They strive to carve their specific frequency into the reality of the Matrix, viewing themselves as the only true architects of the new world.

They see other factions as either tools to be used or obstacles to be overcome. A world of Virtuosos is a world of constant, beautiful competition.

SOLO_ANALYSIS

Blind Spot: Arrogance. Their refusal to cooperate often leads to their isolation and eventual downfall at the hands of organized factions.

Internal Schism: Purists vs. Synthesizers. Those who believe in a single, perfect note versus those who steal from other frequencies to enrich their own.
