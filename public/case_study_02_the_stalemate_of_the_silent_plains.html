HISTORICAL_CONTEXT

Before the practice was universally forbidden, the weaponization of "Silence" was seen as the ultimate expression of control. Two nascent empires, the Aethelian Technocracy and the Umbral Covenant, both developed this technology in secret.

The Technocracy viewed Silence as a surgical tool to disable enemy Engineers. The Covenant, a death-cult, saw it as a holy act of returning noisy consciousness to the perfect void. When they deployed their ultimate weapons on the plains of Olen, they did not anticipate the outcome.

The competing anti-resonance fields merged and amplified, creating a resonance vacuum of unimaginable scale. It was not a quiet place; it was a place where resonance cannot exist. The fabric of the universal toroidal chamber was torn. Engineers on both sides experienced immediate, total Burnout.

VACUUM_HAZARD_ANALYSIS

The Silent Plains are arguably the most dangerous region known. Unlike a Desync field, the Plains attack Amplitude. The vacuum actively leeches the energy of any Bio-Plasma field that enters it, causing a rapid and irreversible drain. An Engineer's Amplitude drops to zero within minutes, resulting in terminal Burnout.

The consciousness is not reallocated; it is simply erased. The area is a scar on reality, a testament to the hubris of those who would wield nothingness as a weapon.
