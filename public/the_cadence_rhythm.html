PHILOSOPHY_OF_STRUCTURE

The philosophy of the Cadence is one of pragmatic structuralism. We believe the Resonance Matrix is governed by underlying laws, the most fundamental of which is Rhythm.

For us, power and societal health are derived from discipline, hierarchy, and the unwavering execution of a clear, unified purpose. We are the soldiers, the builders, and the guardians of order.

We see the intricate solos of Melody as self-indulgent, the forced unity of Harmony as weak, and the chaos of Noise as an existential threat.

INTERNAL_ANALYSIS

Internal Schism: Traditionalists vs. Polyrhythmics. The battle over whether society should have one single beat or a weave of many.
