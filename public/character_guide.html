Character Guide

KEEPING TIME: Volume 1
TARO NISHIMURA
"The Silence" VOID
Class Null/Void (Unregistered)
Waveform Absence
Affiliation Malkuth Academy (Former)
The protagonist. Initially misclassified as a Rhythm Operator, Taro is revealed to be "The Silence": a user who doesn't emit resonance but absorbs it. His overwhelming sensitivity to noise is not a wrong note but THE signature of a living damping field.
Arc: From trying to "fix" himself to accepting his role as the quiet between the notes—the only force that can silence the chaos.
HANA CHORD
"The Reference" SQUARE
Class Alignment Conductor
Waveform Square (Rigid)
Affiliation Malkuth Academy
The unshakeable reference point. Hana processes the world through literal, binary logic. She sees people as data and uses "The Score" to navigate social situations manually. Her Phase Lock ability makes her the team's metronome.
Arc: From seeing the world as pure code to developing "fuzzy logic"—learning to process emotion and chaos without crashing.
VEN (VENTUS)
"The Sink" BASS
Class Structural Architect (Tank)
Waveform Sub-Bass
Affiliation Malkuth Academy
The silent giant. Ven absorbs excess resonance from the team, acting as a thermal "Ground State." He rarely speaks, channeling overwhelming power into the earth through his connection to low-frequency architecture.
Arc: Learning that being the foundation doesn't mean being invisible—finding his voice while carrying the weight.
ADJUTANT VOSS
"The Admin" SINE
Class Admin (Architect)
Waveform Pure Sine (Artificial)
Affiliation C-Order
The Headmaster of Malkuth Academy. Voss doesn't "resonate"—he edits. As an Admin-class user, he can see and manipulate the raw code behind reality. He views Dissonance as corruption to be debugged.
Arc: The enforcer of "stability." He must reckon with whether his perfect order is harmony or stagnation.
ELARA (KIMURA)
"The Wireframe" TREBLE
Class Structural Architect (Modulated)
Waveform Treble Construct (Glass)
Affiliation The Underground (The Null)
Assigned the "Tank" class at birth, Elara suffers from Resonance Dysphoria—her soul-frequency doesn't match her imposed hull. She illegally modulates herself into a high-fidelity treble construct: slender, glass-winged, and finally right.
Arc: From being trapped in the "Kimura" hull to teaching Taro that "wrong noting" is just "modulating without permission."
NYX FEEDBACK
"The Dissonant Overtone" STATIC
Class Disruption Specialist
Waveform Out-of-Phase
Affiliation E Flux Syndicate (Loose)
Born wrong—or made wrong. Nyx exists slightly out of phase with reality, causing feedback and instability wherever she goes. She's nearly impossible to hit, but her presence makes resonance unreliable for everyone nearby.
Arc: From weaponizing her loneliness to realizing that Dissonance isn't destruction—it's counterpoint.
