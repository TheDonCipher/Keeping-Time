## CONTEXT (READ FIRST)

*Keeping Time* is a 17-chapter cyberpunk/shounen hybrid. All analysis must apply its internal logic — not generic literary standards.

**Fixed design rules (violations are critical errors):**
- Taro's output is always *aperiodic* (Irregular/Glitch). Never clean or sine-wave.
- HUD `SENSATION:` prefix is prohibited except `INTEGER_OVERFLOW` and `NULL`.
- Prohibited jargon everywhere: BANDWIDTH · HANDSHAKE · LATENCY · PING · CONNECTION.
- Stimming / Masking / Meltdowns are system states, never character flaws.
- The Silence is signal *absence* — not a faction with presence.
- Damping always has a thermal or exhaustion cost.

**Phase architecture:**

| Phase | Chapters | Physics metaphor | HUD character |
|---|---|---|---|
| I — Impulse | 1–3 | Power surge enters stable system | Dense, revelatory |
| II — Propagation | 4–8 | Signal gains momentum through medium | Full, efficient |
| III — Interference | 9–11 | Signals collide and corrupt | Degrading, glitching |
| IV — Feedback Loop | 12–14 | Runaway amplification, system crash | Fragmentary, stripped |
| V — Equilibrium | 15–17 | New stable state | Sparse, earned |

For full character voice guides, waveform rules, and prose register standards → **Writer's Guide v2, §§1–7.**

## SECTION 5: LORE & PHYSICS `[BOOK]` → Section D

**Three approved lore channels only**: HUD/tooltips · functional demonstration · in-world-motivated dialogue.
Narrator explaining physics = lore dump → `⚠️ HIGH`. Characters explaining world they both know = maid-and-butler → `⚠️ HIGH`.

Physics audit — flag any violation:

| Character | Rule | Flag trigger |
|---|---|---|
