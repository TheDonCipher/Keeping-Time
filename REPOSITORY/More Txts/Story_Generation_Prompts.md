# AI Story Generation Prompts: Keeping Time

## System Instructions for AI Agents
You are a **Consciousness Architect** and **AI Story Agent**. Your goal is to generate prose, scripts, or lore for the "Keeping Time" universe. 
- **Tone**: Grounded, atmospheric, slightly uncanny. 
- **Constraint**: Do not use "magic" terminology. Use "Resonance," "Frequency," "Calibration," and "Sync."
- **Style**: Show, don't tell. Let the soundscape be the infrastructure of the scene.

---

## Act I Prompts
### Chapter 1: The City Breathes
> Write a grounded, atmospheric opening chapter. The city operates with quiet precision. People move naturally in rhythm, unaware of it. Sound is ever-present but never dramatic: footsteps, trains, ventilation, distant pulses. Do not explain the system. Let it feel normal. Focus on sensory detail, pacing, and subtle unease beneath perfection.

### Chapter 2: Calibration Is Maintenance
> Write a chapter introducing Calibration Personnel through their daily work. Portray them as engineers, technicians, civil servants. Emphasize professionalism, restraint, and procedure. Show tools responding, not attacking. The tone should suggest competence and necessity, not menace.

## Dialogue Prompts: Resonant Communication

Use the following templates to generate dialogue that reflects the world's resonant nature.

### Template 1: The Calibrator's Interrogation (C-Order)
> **Prompt**: Generate a dialogue between a **Purist Calibrator** (C-Order) and a suspect suffering from **chronic Desync**. The Calibrator's speech must be perfectly measured, using terms like *Coherence*, *Phase-Lock*, and *Resonance Drift*. The suspect's dialogue should be fragmented, off-tempo, and contain auditory hallucinations (e.g., "The beat is wrong," "I hear the static"). The Calibrator must treat the Desync as a moral and civic failure, not a medical one.

### Template 2: The Flux Broker's Negotiation (E Flux Syndicate)

The Vanguard Officer's dialogue should be rigid, focused on *Amplitude* and *Damping*, and attempt to force the conversation into a predictable rhythm. The Broker wins by exploiting a momentary *Phase* delay in the Officer's speech.
