*Theme: The system notices an anomaly.*
- **Ch 5-8**: Introduce the protagonist as an "outlier" who is unaffected by desynchronization. The system begins to self-protect through institutional denial.

### **Act III: Fractures**
*Theme: The system strains.*
- **Ch 9-12**: Escalate stakes as Low-Fi zones persist. Introduce forbidden terminology and the first clear resistance from the outlier.

### **Act IV: Keeping Time**
*Theme: Authority vs. Inevitability.*
- **Ch 13-16**: The system intensifies its correction efforts. A senior calibrator realizes the truth: the system is being outpaced. The outlier is officially "erased."

### **Act V: Resonance**
*Theme: Identity without permission.*
- **Ch 17-20**: The protagonist learns through sensation and breath. A Low-Fi zone stabilizes naturally. Ends on the introduction of "Control of Calibration" and lingering uncertainty.
