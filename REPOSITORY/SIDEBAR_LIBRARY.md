### FAILURE MODES

#### Desync
<aside>
<details>
<summary><strong>SUBJECT: Desync Event</strong></summary>
<blockquote>
<strong>Pathology:</strong> Occurs when a user's internal frequency drifts too far from the local environmental baseline.
<br><br>
<strong>Symptoms:</strong> Nausea, "lag" in motor control, sensory artifacting (seeing double/pixels), and inability to execute Command Syntax.
<br><br>
<strong>- Safety Pamphlet 101</strong>
</blockquote>
</details>
</aside>

#### Burnout
<aside>
<details>
<summary><strong>SUBJECT: Burnout (Neural Scarring)</strong></summary>
<blockquote>
<strong>Pathology:</strong> Permanent reduction in Bio-Plasma capacity caused by channeling Amplitude beyond safe limits (Overclocking).
<br><br>
<strong>Prognosis:</strong> Irreversible. Cumulative burnout results in "Hollowing"—the total loss of ability to interface with the Medium.
<br><br>
<strong>- Warning Label: Class A Amplifiers</strong>
</blockquote>
</details>
</aside>
