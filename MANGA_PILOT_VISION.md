# MANGA PILOT: THE ZAP (CHAPTER 0)
## Visual Storyboard & Prompt Map

This document translates the prose of Chapter 1 into a fast-paced 10-panel pilot for Webtoon/Manga platforms.

---

### PANEL 1: THE CLOCK
- **Visual**: Macro close-up of Taro's eye. A digital HUD reflects in his pupil.
- **Text**: `> SYSTEM: BOOTING...`
- **Art Prompt**: `Extreme close-up, human eye, digital interface reflection, high-tech HUD overlay, cyan and amber lighting, hyper-realistic, 8k --ar 16:9`

### PANEL 2: THE CLINIC
- **Visual**: Wide shot of the sterile, white Unison clinic. Taro sits on a bench, looking small.
- **Text**: "I can feel the static building."
- **Art Prompt**: `Wide shot, futuristic hospital room, sterile, minimal, white and gold aesthetic, Swiss design architecture, cinematic lighting --ar 3:2`

### PANEL 3: THE DOCTOR
- **Visual**: Dr. Vere holding a calibration band. Her tag reads `[STATUS: CALIBRATOR]`.
- **Text**: "Taro, stay in the grid. Don't drift."
- **Art Prompt**: `Female doctor, futuristic medical suit, holding glowing silicone bracelet, holographic interface tags, clean lines, anime style --ar 4:5`

### PANEL 4: THE ZAP (SPLASH)
- **Visual**: Taro's body arching. White light erupting from his chest in a sound-wave pattern. The world begins to turn into a wireframe.
- **Art Prompt**: `Manga splash page, boy experiencing energy surge, white-hot bio-plasma, concentric sound wave ripples, world turning into digital wireframe, high energy, dynamic pose --ar 1:1`

### PANEL 5: THE GLITCH
- **Visual**: Close-up of the HUD. A gold spike breaks the blue grid. `> ERROR: BUFFER_OVERFLOW`.
- **Art Prompt**: `UI failure, digital glitch, distorted text, gold energy sparks, motherboard pattern, chaotic data stream --ar 16:9`
